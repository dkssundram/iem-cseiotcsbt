About

The Computer Science & Engineering Department is one of the oldest engineering departments of Institute of Engineering and Management. IEM has been ranked ‘A’ Category by NAAC. CSE Department was established in the year 1997 under the affiliation of Kalyani University and subsequently under Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (erstwhile West Bengal University of Technology). Right from its inception, it aimed towards imparting quality education with special emphasis on technical knowledge. With access to the research in cutting edge technology, students from this department have maintained a consistent balance between academic excellence and extracurricular activities which is reflected in their achievements in the technical fests, science exhibitions, cultural fests, etc.

Vision

Evolve as a leading academic unit of excellence emphasizing on outcome and innovation based education with international reputation and adopt itself to the rapid advancement in the field of Computer Science & Engineering and related disciplines.

Mission

M1: To educate our students so that they will reach their full potential in computer science and engineering research and professional practice through an in-depth understanding of the fundamentals and developing an interest for lifelong learning.
M2: To encourage the teachers to adapt to the innovative teaching learning process focusing on example based teaching in line with contemporary trends and feedbacks from all Stakeholders.
M3: To promote a culture of entrepreneurship, innovation and start-ups among the students.
M4: To encourage the students to imbibe the concept of corporate social responsibility.
